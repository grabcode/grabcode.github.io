## The Vision

A digital registration platform built for **Ticketek** to manage signups for the **World War commemoration events** — connecting veterans and their families with ceremonies honouring their service.

## My Role & Impact

As freelance Tech Lead, I designed and built the signup experience, ensuring it was **accessible, reliable, and respectful** of the occasion. The platform needed to handle sensitive personal data and high traffic volumes during registration windows.

- Led the design and development of the registration system
- Built accessible, responsive forms for veterans and families
- Ensured reliability under traffic surges during sign-up periods
- Worked closely with Ticketek's team to meet event deadlines

## Key Features

- **Registration system**: Streamlined signup for veterans and families
- **Accessible design**: Built with inclusivity in mind for an older demographic
- **Responsive**: Works across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Data handling**: Secure management of personal information
