## The Vision

Liftango provides **turn-key solutions for Shared Mobility** — whether On-Demand Transport, Corporate Carpooling, or Transit Tracking. We built the technology platform that powered shared rides for hundreds of thousands of users across government and enterprise clients.

## My Role & Impact

As co-founder, CPO, and CTO, I wore every hat from product vision to code to hiring. Over 5+ years, I:

- **Served hundreds of thousands** of users' shared rides
- **Delivered solutions for tens of clients** across government and enterprise
- Met **strict due diligence** expectations for multi-million dollar financing rounds
- Developed the **first versions of every core business product** from scratch, on time and on budget
- Won **Government and Local awards** for Best Cloud Solution and Mobile Apps

## Tech Stack

A bunch of really cool tech, highly scalable and cost-effective.

## Key Features

- **On-Demand Transport**: Real-time ride matching and dispatch
- **Corporate Carpooling**: Enterprise solutions for employee commute optimisation
- **Transit Tracking**: Live vehicle tracking and passenger information
- **Multi-tenant Platform**: White-label solutions for diverse client needs
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Operational insights and reporting for clients
- **Payment Integration**: Seamless fare management and settlements

## The Journey

Liftango was my most substantial startup venture — 5 years of building a company from the ground up, scaling both the product and the team internationally. It taught me a lot about the intersection of technology leadership, product strategy, and company building. The skills I developed here — scaling teams, meeting enterprise-grade requirements, and shipping reliable products — directly prepared me for my current role in engineering management.
