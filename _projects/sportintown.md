## The Vision

SportInTown was a community-driven platform that solved a fundamental problem: finding people to play sports with in your local area. Whether you wanted to join a pickup basketball game, find a tennis partner, or organize a weekend hiking group, SportInTown connected you with fellow enthusiasts nearby. It bridged the gap between solo athletes and organized teams, making it easy to discover and participate in sports activities within your community.

## My Role & Impact

As a full-stack developer on the SportInTown team, I contributed to building the core platform infrastructure that powered community discovery and activity coordination. My work focused on ensuring reliable, scalable features that helped users find sports partners and activities, with an emphasis on real-time updates and seamless user experience.

## Tech Stack

A bunch of really cool tech, highly scalable and cost-effective.

## Key Features

- **Local activity discovery**: Browse sports activities happening near you
- **Community profiles**: Showcase your athletic interests and skill levels
- **Activity creation**: Organize pickup games and sports events
- **Matchmaking**: Get matched with compatible players and skill levels
- **Real-time notifications**: Instant updates on activity requests and confirmations
- **Review system**: Build community trust through activity feedback

## The Impact

SportInTown represented the collaborative potential of web technology to bring communities together. Working on this project deepened my understanding of building platforms that facilitate real-world human connections and the technical challenges of handling real-time coordination at scale.
