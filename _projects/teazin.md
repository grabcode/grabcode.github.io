## The Vision

Teazin was a playful social application designed to make modern dating and social interaction more fun and engaging. Built around the concept of lighthearted flirtation and connection, it offered a fresh take on how people could interact, express interest, and build relationships in the mobile-first era. The platform emphasized the social and entertaining aspects of meeting new people, moving beyond traditional dating paradigms.

## My Role & Impact

As a full-stack developer on the Teazin team, I built a prototype that demonstrated the strengths and limitations of hybrid technologies at the time.


## The Learning

Teazin was my first attempt at cofounding a business. It was a complex endeavour with too many technical obstacles. However, I've met some brilliant minds, inspiring me to keep going.
