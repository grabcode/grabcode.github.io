## The Vision

Radar is a **dark web scraping platform** built for Redbelts, an Australian cybersecurity firm. It continuously monitors dark web marketplaces and forums, identifying threats and data breaches relevant to clients — and launching real-time alerts to Chief Security Officers before damage is done.

## My Role & Impact

As freelance Tech Lead, I **led the creation of the platform from scratch**, coordinating a small team of contractors. I designed the scraping architecture, defined the alert pipeline, and ensured the system could operate reliably in the unpredictable landscape of dark web sources.

- Architected the scraping and indexing engine
- Built real-time alerting system for CSOs
- Led a team of contractors through design to delivery
- Navigated the unique technical challenges of dark web data collection

## Key Features

- **Dark web monitoring**: Automated scraping of marketplaces, forums, and paste sites
- **Threat identification**: Pattern matching and keyword detection for client-specific risks
- **Real-time alerts**: Instant notifications to Chief Security Officers
- **Reporting dashboard**: Aggregated threat intelligence for executive briefings
