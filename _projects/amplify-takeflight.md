## The Vision

Amplify TakeFlight is a **health tracking Android app** built for CSC (now DXC Technology). Using **Bluetooth Beacon technology**, it detects when employees choose the stairs over the elevator, tracks their effort, and rewards them through a gamified ladder system — turning healthy choices into a company-wide challenge.

## My Role & Impact

As freelance Tech Lead, I led the end-to-end creation of the app with a small team of contractors. I designed the beacon detection system, the gamification mechanics, and the Android app architecture.

- Architected the Bluetooth Beacon proximity detection system
- Designed the gamification and reward ladder
- Led a team of contractors through design, development, and delivery
- Integrated with CSC's internal systems for employee profiles

## Tech Stack

- **Android (Java)**: Native app for reliable beacon detection
- **Bluetooth Beacons**: iBeacon/Eddystone for stairwell proximity sensing
- **Gamification Engine**: Points, streaks, and ladder-based rewards
- **Health Tracking**: Step and stair-climb activity logging

## Key Features

- **Beacon detection**: Automatically recognises when employees use stairs
- **Effort tracking**: Logs stair climbs and calculates health metrics
- **Reward ladder**: Gamified progression system with milestones and rewards
- **Leaderboards**: Company-wide and team-based competition
- **Push notifications**: Encouragement and achievement alerts
